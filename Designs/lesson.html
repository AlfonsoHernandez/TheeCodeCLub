First Website

HTML

Description

Build a website about anything you want! Make sure to choose an intersting topic that will attract people to your website. Must include at least 4 images and 4 small paragraphs. Also include h tags for titles and subtitles within your website.

Requirements

  • At least 4 images
  • At least 4 short paragraphs
  • Title for each image
